Oracle Predictor

Oracle Performance Overview

The oracle predictor is conditioned on a specific hop radius k and only considers spins within that distance from the target node. It computes exact energy changes within this constrained neighborhood.

ΔE = -si ∑j: d(i,j)≤k sj Jij
